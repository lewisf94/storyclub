StoryClub

About & Credits

StoryClub is a free hobby project, supported by optional donations.

StoryClub is a book-club app: a group adds books to a wheel, takes turns spinning to pick the month's book, sets a read-by deadline, then everyone marks it read and leaves a sealed half-star rating and review. Reviews unseal and the turn passes once every member has read and rated.

It's built and run by Lewis Fowler. Questions, feedback or bug reports are welcome at your-contact@example.com.

Book data & credits

Book covers, titles, authors, publication years, page counts and subjects are provided by Open Library, a project of the Internet Archive. Open Library's catalogue is community-edited and openly licensed; all rights in that data remain with their respective owners.
